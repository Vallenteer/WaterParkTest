About

Waters of Wonder is a themed waterpark in Bintan Island as an addition to Bintan’s tourist attractions that serves domestic and international visitors and as a boost to local tourism.

Waters of Wonder, or “WOW” for short, is inspired by the enchanting Garden of Eden, which would be represented by the amount of the lushious tropical floras that is planned to surround the area of our water slides, pools, and other recreational facilities.

Our goal is to create a paradise-like recreational space that would make our visitors filled with excitement from the fun-in-the-sun ambience and our exhilarating attractions.

Products

We provide visitors with products, facilities, and services to maximize the WOW experience.

  • Rides and Attractions

    Our waterpark has 15 individually unique water slides, wave pool, leisure pool with sun bathing area, pool bar, and lazy river

  • Gazebos

    Gazebos are provided to give visitors the ideal home base for the day for IDR 200.000.

  • Locker Room

    Lockers are provided to protect your valuables for convenience and safety for IDR 20.000.

  • Food Court

    Feel refreshed and rejuvenated after having fun-in-the-sun at our food court that serves both traditional and international delicacies suitable for every palate.

  • Digital Photo

    WOur pool attendants are armed with pocket cameras to ensure that they capture visitors’ experiences and moments.

  • First Aid Room

    Our waterpark has an on site first aid room to handle minor care emergencies.
